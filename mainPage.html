Summary of the Olympics

St.Louis 1904 Summer Olympics

The first Olympics ever hosted by the United States. Those olympics are famous primarily for the marathone, during which a lot of participants were heavily injured. The winner of the marathone set the slowest record time in the Olympics history of 3 hours and 29 minutes

Munich 1972 Summer Olympics

Initially known as "The Cheerful Games", due to the fact that it was conducted during the cold war and near the border between the First and the Second worlds. But, sadly, this event was interrupted by the a terorist group called Black Sepmtember. 11 people died during the terorist attack and the game was interrupted for 18 hours

Barcelona 1992 Summer Olympics

The Summer Olympics of 1992 came at a time of reunification on a global level. The Berlin wall fell just 3 years prior, the iron curtain across Europe started to collapse and South Africa put an end to Aparthite and were now allowed to participate in the games! As such, these games are a major event you must read about!
